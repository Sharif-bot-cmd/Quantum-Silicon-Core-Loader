# LEGAL COUNSEL FRAMEWORK & PRO BONO SUPPORT

## Legal Philosophy
This project operates on principles of:
- **Proactive Compliance**: Meeting legal requirements before issues arise
- **Transparent Documentation**: Clear public legal positioning
- **Ethical Boundaries**: Self-imposed restrictions beyond legal minimums
- **Community Accountability**: Public scrutiny as safeguard

## Pro Bono Legal Support
We welcome pro bono assistance from:
- **Digital Rights Organizations** (EFF, ACLU, etc.)
- **Academic Law Clinics** (University legal aid)
- **Public Interest Law Firms**
- **Human Rights Advocates**

## Legal Contact Protocol
For legitimate legal inquiries:
1. Must provide specific legal basis for claims
2. PGP-encrypted communication preferred
3. Public discussion encouraged for transparency
4. Philippine jurisdiction acknowledged

## Defense Strategy Layers
1. **Preventive**: Comprehensive documentation (this repository)
2. **Reactive**: Community and organizational support
3. **Appellate**: Academic and public advocacy
4. **Strategic**: Media and political narrative control

## International Legal Positioning
- **Philippine Law**: Primary jurisdiction and compliance
- **International Law**: UN principles and human rights frameworks
- **Comparative Law**: Right to repair precedents globally
- **Ethical Law**: Beyond legal minimum to ethical maximum
